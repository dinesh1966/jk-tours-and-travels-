Top Tour Places

Kodaikanal Lake

Famous star-shaped man-made lake surrounded by lush greenery, perfect for boating.

Coaker's Walk

A scenic paved pedestrian path overlooking the plains below with breathtaking views.

Bryant Park

A beautifully maintained botanical park with exotic flowers and rare plant species.

Pillar Rocks

Three giant rock pillars standing tall at 400 feet high with misty surroundings.

Guna Caves

Also known as Devil's Kitchen, these mysterious caves are surrounded by dense forest.

Pine Forest

A serene dense pine forest ideal for nature walks and photography.

Silver Cascade Falls

A stunning 180-foot waterfall cascading down rocky cliffs on the way to Kodaikanal.

Moir Point

A scenic viewpoint offering panoramic views of the valley and surrounding hills.

Green Valley View

A mesmerizing viewpoint overlooking the deep valley covered in lush green forest.
