Top Tour Places

Adiyogi Shiva Statue

The iconic 112-foot bust of Lord Shiva at Isha Foundation.

Marudhamalai Temple

Sacred hill temple dedicated to Lord Murugan with stunning views.

Perur Pateeswarar Temple

Ancient Shiva temple known for its Natarajar shrine and rich history.

VOC Park and Zoo

A popular zoo and park with diverse wildlife and lush greenery.

Kovai Kutralam Falls

A beautiful waterfall nestled in the Siruvani hills, perfect for a refreshing dip.

Gedee Car Museum

A unique museum showcasing vintage and classic cars from around the world.

Valparai

A serene hill station with tea and coffee plantations amidst misty mountains.

Topslip

A wildlife sanctuary and eco-tourism spot in the Anamalai Hills.

Aliyar Dam

A picturesque dam surrounded by hills, ideal for boating and picnics.

Monkey Falls

A stunning waterfall on the Pollachi-Valparai road surrounded by dense forest.
