Top Tour Places

Coorg

Coffee estates, waterfalls, and misty hills — the Scotland of India.

Mysore Palace

One of India's most beautiful royal palaces with stunning Indo-Saracenic architecture.

Chikmagalur

Mountain views, trekking, and coffee plantations in the Western Ghats.

Hampi

Ancient temples and historic ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire, a UNESCO site.

Jog Falls

One of India's highest waterfalls, a breathtaking 830-foot plunge.

Gokarna

Pristine beaches, ancient temples, and serene coastal scenery.

Bandipur National Park

Wildlife safaris and nature tours in one of India's premier tiger reserves.

Udupi

Beaches, temples, and local cuisine — the temple town of Karnataka.

Murudeshwar Temple

Famous for the world's second tallest Shiva statue overlooking the Arabian Sea.
