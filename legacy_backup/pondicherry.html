Top Tour Places

Paradise Beach

A beautiful pristine beach accessible only by boat, offering golden sands.

Rock Beach

A picturesque rocky shore popular for evening walks and sunrise views.

Promenade Beach

The iconic long beachfront promenade stretching along the city coast.

Auroville & Matrimandir

The universal township and its spectacular golden globe meditation center.

Sri Aurobindo Ashram

A spiritual community founded by Sri Aurobindo and The Mother.

Manakula Vinayagar Temple

An ancient Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha, older than the French settlement.

French Colony (White Town)

Walk through heritage streets showcasing beautiful French architecture and cafes.
